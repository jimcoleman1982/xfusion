We started xFusion because we hated what staffing agencies do to people.

In 2020, we looked at the outsourcing industry and saw a broken model. Agencies that bragged about cheap labor. Workers paid so poorly they had no reason to stay longer than a few months. Clients stuck in an endless cycle of hiring, training, losing, and starting over.

We asked a question that seemed obvious: what if you actually treated overseas talent like the skilled professionals they are?

So that's what we built. A company where team members earn real wages, get genuine investment in their growth, and feel like they belong to something. Happy people do better work. They stay longer. They care more. And our clients, from SaaS startups to HVAC companies to law firms, have built teams they trust because they built those teams with us.
