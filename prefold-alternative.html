What changed and why

David cited the Hormozi offer articulation formula: "I solve [A problem] for [B audience] without [C headaches] and with [D guarantee]." Here is how the new hero maps to that formula.

Current Hero

  • Leads with pain but never states the problem we solve
  • Audience is implied, never explicitly called out
  • Headaches we eliminate are not mentioned
  • No guarantee or risk reversal in the hero

Hormozi A/B/C/D Formula

  • [A] Problem: Headline states what we solve: team members who stay, no bad hires
  • [B] Audience: Prehead directly calls out "business owners ready to stop wearing every hat"
  • [C] Without: "No sifting through applicants. No training someone who leaves in three months."
  • [D] Guarantee: "$500 fully refundable. ~14 days. 90%+ retention across 350+ placements."
